About Dr.A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Known as the 'Missile Man of India'for his work on the development
of ballistic missile and launch vehicle technilogy,he was alos a beloved figure for his humility and inspirational speeches.
